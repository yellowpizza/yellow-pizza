    About Us

Yellow Pizza offers pizza made with fresh ingredients for taste. This delightful pizzeria was made by Oliver, who has experience making Pizza for over 30 years in New York at Yellow Pizza. The rich flavor of freshly bought tomatoes, olives, and cheese make for a perfect pizza.

Whether you are looking for a quick pizza lunch, or looking to bring your family and have a good time, we have the pizza for you. Stop by at our New York location, or visit our other locations throughout the states. Be sure to try all of our amazing flavors. You can also build your own pizza! We are open from Tuesdays to Thursdays, 12PM to 8PM.
